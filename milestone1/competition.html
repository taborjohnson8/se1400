Competition

Competition Information:

What is my competition doing right now?

My competition is hosting their content on multiple different sites. Many having different capabilities on each site.

What is my competition doing right?

My competition is hosting their content on platforms that have many mutual creators. This allows them to host their content in communities related to their field.

What is my competition doing wrong?

My competition is often spreading their content through multiple platforms that limit them from how they display their work.

How can I do it better?

Being able to host my content on my own site will give me the flexibility of how I want to display it. I will also be able to have any asset store, contact information, and related content hosted on one site.
